The Best Movies According to Taimoor!

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter

A young wizard named Harry Potter embarks on a journey to defeat the dark wizard Voldemort, with the help of his friends and the guidance of his mentors.

Vikings

Vikings were seafaring Norse warriors and traders from Scandinavia who explored, raided, and settled across Europe from the late 8th to early 11th centuries.

Peaky Blinders

"Peaky Blinders" is a British crime drama series that follows the exploits of the Shelby crime family in post-World War I Birmingham, led by the ambitious and cunning Tommy Shelby.
